# Apollo Email Outreach to Google Employees

**Goal:** Build an email outreach campaign for Apollo to acquire email addresses from Google employees.

## Strategic Overview:

The objective is to effectively engage Google employees through a targeted email outreach campaign using Apollo, ultimately securing their professional email addresses for further engagement. The core strategy involves hyper-targeting, a value-first approach, personalization at scale, clear and low-friction calls to action, and a multi-step sequence.

## Agent Tasks:

### Outbound Strategist (Focus: Planning & Messaging)

1.  **Define Target Personas (Google Employees):** Identify 2-3 specific Google employee roles/departments, outlining their pain points, goals, and how Apollo addresses them.
2.  **Develop Core Value Proposition & Messaging Framework:** Craft a compelling core value proposition for Apollo tailored to Google employees and develop a messaging framework.
3.  **Outline Outreach Sequence Strategy:** Propose a multi-step email outreach sequence (e.g., Initial Contact, Follow-up 1, Follow-up 2), defining purpose, content themes, and timing.
4.  **Suggest Personalization Angles:** Identify specific data points or approaches for personalizing emails for Google employees.

### Sales Outreach (Focus: Execution & Copywriting)

1.  **Draft Email Templates (3-4 variations):** Draft 3-4 distinct email templates based on the strategy, including initial contact and follow-ups.
2.  **Incorporate Personalization Placeholders:** Ensure templates include clear placeholders for personalization (e.g., `{{first_name}}`, `{{company_name}}`).
3.  **Craft Compelling Subject Lines:** Develop 5-7 engaging and open-rate-optimized subject lines.
4.  **Define Clear Call to Action (CTA):** Ensure each email template has a single, clear, low-friction CTA aimed at obtaining the Google employee's email or initiating a conversation.
